Understanding how your app’s Store assets impact user behavior can help you make a great first impression and drive traffic to your product detail page (PDP). Explore new tools that let you experiment with assets—and quickly get clarity on their effectiveness.
Drive and measure PDP conversion
with A/B testing
The A/B Testing Tool enables you to test two versions of PDP assets like Cover Art, Video Trailers and more—and compare results in the Developer Dashboard. Use this tool to test your hypothesis, inform your strategy and drive conversion.

Check out our case study to learn how the developers of FitXR, Synth Riders, Vacation Simulator and Spacefolk City increased or validated conversion rates with A/B testing.

Minimize risk with automatic metadata
A/B testing
While the A/B Testing Tool lets you test PDP assets independently, our new Release Holdout feature lets you test changes systematically to minimize the risk of reduced performance.

This optional feature lets you test metadata updates with 50% of your audience to evaluate performance—and automatically reverts your PDP to previous metadata if a significant drop in sales occurs (3% or more).

Immersive Image Layers
Immersive Image Layers are a new type of Store asset that adds flavor and personality to your app’s PDP in-headset. These image layers highlight objects, characters and backgrounds from your title to create an immersive, 3D Store experience that gives audiences a sneak peek of your offering.
